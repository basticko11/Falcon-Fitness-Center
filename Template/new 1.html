Motivational Quotes

First Quote

Carpe Diem

Meaning

This quote is Latin and means Seize the Day. It's a proclamation to take control of your day
and make the most each hour, minute, and second. You can either claim the day or the day can claim you.

Second Quote

You miss 100 percent of the shots you don't take- Wayne Gretzky

Meaning

You're never going to accomplish something if you never take a shot at it. Failure to make the effort
to achieve something is the equivalent of not even desiring anything at all. If you want something in this life,
you have to have the courage to go for it.
